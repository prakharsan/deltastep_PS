About Us



We at DeltaStep understand that just like every child has a unique face, unique fingerprint; she has a unique learning ability as well. Hence, DeltaStep is an interactive, adaptive and highly personalized learning platform that enables the student to learn anytime, anywhere - at her own pace and in her own style.

Features

Through it's innovative and unique features, DeltaStep combines the best of both the worlds, classroom learning and self study

Engaging Lectures

Enquiry based learning through real life applications; brings about conceptual clarity and creates interest.

Powerful video tools

Speed up or slow down the video; change the camera options

Interactive Lectures

Periodic questions asked in a timed manner to measure understanding and attentiveness.

Cross-curricular linking

E-library of concepts and cross-linking of earlier concepts as instant references

Games and Simulations

Play your way through difficult concepts by playing games mapped to the school curriculum.

Adaptive Practice Modules

The difficulty level of the next question depends on the response to the current one ensuring that the student progresses in an order of difficulty

Round the clock support

Proactive doubt-detecting intelligence with prompt, tailor-made doubt-clearing mechanism.
